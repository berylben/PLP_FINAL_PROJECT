About Villa Rehabilitation Center.

Villa Rehabilitation center was first founded in 2005 by the Peterson's.Initially ,it was meant to be a home for the old people and a separate wing for people with diseases such as Parkistons ans like which are mostly associated with the old people in the society.It was founded in Winsconsin state ,in the USA.The main reason as to why the center was laterchanged to a rehabilitation center was that the neighbouring society and community at large was adversely affected by drugs abuse and like addictions.The Petersons decided to change the center to be a rehab center so as to help change the addicted persons in the society at that time. Years later ,the rehabilitation center is home to hundreds of addicts who wish to get a second shot at life and change their lives for the better.
