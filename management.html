The Board Of Management

With commitment and dedication of the Board of Directors, executives, and employees as mentioned above, including good collaboration of business partners, trust from customers, and supports of various agencies, it resulted in the Company grow continuously and meet the targeted results. For the year 2020, the Company posted a revenue of Baht 1,321.5 million, increasing by 1% from the previous year. EBITDA and net profit were Baht 635.6 million and Baht 95.2 million, increasing from the year 2019 by 10.7% and 113% respectively. Earnings per share was Baht 0.22. The board of directors of Villa Rehabilitation Center has the needs of the people at heart and do all they can to make it easier for those struggling with addictions.
